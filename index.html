Software Development Engineer
Level I, Level II

I began my software engineer in the real world adventures at Chef during the summer of 2015.
I began in Javascript, helping to build the UI for our enterprise product.
I familiarized myself with InSpec and started giving presentations.

After proving my skillset in learning new technologies and executing on goals, I moved to working in go, which has been my focus for the last year and a half.
As a go developer, I designed and implemented a scheduled infrastructure scanner service for our enterprise product.

Once the scanner service proved itself stable, I was given the responsibility of extending the scanner service to work with cloud integrations (aws and azure). The end result is the ability auto-discovery of nodes in cloud services, extended filtering mechanisms for sorting through that infrastructure, and the ability to scan the nodes with InSpec without providing credentials.

Engineering Lead

As an Engineering Lead at Chef Software, I am responsible for the execution of our team goals.
I lead the daily standup, prioritize the work in accordance with product and the engineering directors, am responsible for ensuring my team members are unblocked and enabled in their work, and continue to contribute to the code base and product development.

I am the gateway that declares whether my domain of our enterprise product (the compliance section) is ready for release during each release cycle (weekly) and communicate with customers to debug issues found when necessary.
